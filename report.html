Moving the Dancer

The dancer is drawn with the function draw_dancer(). This function takes an input an array of 40 floats. Each element in the array corresponds to one of the degrees of freedom of the dancer. Here is the mapping of the array elements to the degrees of the freedom that they represent:

Array Elements Joint
0 to 2head-neck joint
3 to 5left shoulder
6 to 8right shoulder
9 to 11neck-torso1 joint
12 to 14torso1-torso2 joint
15 to 17torso2-torso3 joint
18 to 20left hip
21 to 23right hip
24 to 26left ankle
27 to 29right ankle
30 to 32left wrist
33 to 35right wrist
36left knee
37right knee
38left elbow
39right elbow
